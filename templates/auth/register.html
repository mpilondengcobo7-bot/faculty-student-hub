POPIA Declaration
Protection of Personal Information Act (Act 4 of 2013)
Purpose of Collection

Durban University of Technology (DUT) collects your personal information — including your name, email address, student number, and department — solely for the purpose of operating the Faculty–Student Collaborative Project Hub. This information is used to authenticate your identity, facilitate project collaboration, and track academic progress within the platform.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data will be used to create and manage your account, display your profile to fellow participants and faculty, record project milestones, task submissions, and feedback, and issue certificates of completion. Your information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties outside the DUT institution for marketing purposes.

Data Security & Retention

DUT implements reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Your data will be retained for the duration of your registration and may be archived for academic record-keeping purposes in accordance with DUT's data retention policy.

Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. You may contact the DUT Information Officer at informationofficer@dut.ac.za to exercise these rights. You may withdraw consent at any time; however, this may affect your ability to use the platform.

Responsible Party

The responsible party for the processing of your personal information is Durban University of Technology, Steve Biko Campus, Durban, 4001, South Africa.
