How the Reminder Works
Every 4 hours the scheduler scans all tasks whose due date is today.
For each incomplete task, it finds the students who must submit (either the assigned student, or all project participants).
If a student has not yet submitted and has not been reminded in the last 4 hours, they receive an in-app notification and an email.
Once a student submits their work, reminders for that task stop automatically.
Reminders only fire on the due date itself — no advance warnings are sent by this job.
